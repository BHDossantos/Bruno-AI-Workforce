# BnB Global — Go-To-Market & Growth Strategy

**B&B Global Services — "From Idea to Operations."** Full-stack tech consultancy:
Strategy/Fractional-CTO · App & Product Dev · Data/Analytics/AI · Cloud/DevOps/
Platform · DevSecOps/Cybersecurity · Reliability/SRE/BCDR · Managed IT (IMAC).

The unfair advantage: the founder's real track record (Director-level SRE/Cloud
Ops — raised SLA 98.5%→99.95%, cut cloud cost ~30%, led SRE at CVS Health). That
makes **founder-led** outreach credible in a way agencies can't fake.

## 1. Don't sell "consulting" — sell outcomes (wedges)
"Tech consulting" is a commodity. Lead with a single high-pain, high-budget,
fast-ROI wedge, land the relationship, then expand:

| Wedge | Hook | Who feels it |
|---|---|---|
| **Cloud cost cut** | "Cut your AWS/Azure/GCP bill 20–40% — free audit" | Anyone with a 5–7 figure cloud spend |
| **Reliability/SRE** | "Stop the 2am outages — uptime to 99.95%" | Teams with incidents / SLAs |
| **Security/Compliance** | "SOC 2 / security audit-ready in 90 days" | Selling upmarket, vendor reviews |
| **AI enablement** | "Ship your first production GenAI use case in 60 days" | Boards pushing "AI strategy" |
| **Managed IT (IMAC)** | "Onboard/offboard + service desk, handled" | Growing teams, no IT lead |

The cold-email agent picks the **single most relevant wedge per prospect** and
leads with the outcome + one proof point + a soft CTA.

## 2. ICP (two tiers)
- **Tier A — scaling tech**: Series A–C startups & mid-market (50–500 staff)
  scaling infra. Buyers: CTO, VP/Director Eng, Head of Platform/SRE, CISO.
- **Tier B — traditional SMBs**: local businesses needing cloud/security/managed
  IT. Buyers: owner/COO. *(This is what the agent sources today via real local
  business data — immediate, free, deliverable emails.)*

## 3. Offer ladder (the growth engine)
1. **Free 20-min assessment / audit** — the CTA in every message. Qualifies +
   creates urgency, costs nothing, self-evidently valuable.
2. **Fixed-scope sprint** ($10–25k, 2–4 wks) — a fast, contained win.
3. **Fractional CTO / retainer** ($8–20k/mo) — recurring revenue, the real prize.
4. **Project delivery** (app/data/AI builds) — large, follows trust.

## 4. Channels (run by the app)
- **Cold email + follow-ups** — the BnB Global agent, 7 AM daily + the noon/5 PM
  lead passes, with the warmup ramp protecting deliverability.
- **LinkedIn** — the agent drafts a connection note per prospect (assist-send,
  ToS-safe).
- **Authority content** — the social agents post Bruno's POV (reliability, cloud
  cost, AI) to pull inbound.
- **Case studies** — feed wins back into the memory graph so future outreach cites them.

## 5. How it's wired in the app
- **Agent** `bnbglobal` (7 AM) → sources prospects, writes outcome-based email +

1. Point the cold-email mailbox at the BnB Global domain (or use personal with a
